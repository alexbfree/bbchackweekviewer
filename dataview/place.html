Place your data

Now we're ready to analyse your loaded data files and show you some things that are in your data.
Pick out the entities that are meaningful to you and assign them to the area you would like to associate them with.
Any entities you leave unplaced will be ignored for now - don't worry you can always come back and place them or move them later.
